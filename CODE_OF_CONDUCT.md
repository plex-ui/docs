# Code of Conduct

Plex UI follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## TL;DR

- Be kind and be specific.
- Disagree about code, not about people.
- Assume good faith; ask before accusing.
- Private matters go in private channels.

## Unacceptable

- Personal attacks, discrimination, or harassment of any kind.
- Sharing someone else's private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussion.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please email **plexuikit@gmail.com**. Reports are handled confidentially and may result in moderation actions up to and including banning from the project.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, discussions, PRs, Discord, social media interactions that reference the project, and in-person events.

Full text of the Covenant: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
