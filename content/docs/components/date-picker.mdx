### Limits

<ComponentPreview details={<>Use <code>min</code> and <code>max</code> to limit selectable dates to a specific timeframe.</>} code={`const minDate = today.minus({ days: 30 }).startOf('day')
const maxDate = today.plus({ days: 30 }).endOf('day')
<DatePicker value={...} onChange={...} min={minDate} max={maxDate} />`} previewMinHeight={168}>
  <DatePickerLimitsDemo />
</ComponentPreview>

### Date of birth

<ComponentPreview details={<>Set <code>captionLayout="dropdown"</code> to add month and year dropdowns inline with the arrow navigation. Ideal for selecting a date of birth where navigating month-by-month would be impractical.</>} code={`<DatePicker
  captionLayout="dropdown"
  triggerDateFormat="MMMM d, yyyy"
  max={DateTime.local()}
  placeholder="Select your birthday"
  clearable pill
/>`} previewMinHeight={168}>
  <DatePickerDobDemo />
</ComponentPreview>

### Time picker

<ComponentPreview details={<>Enable <code>showTime</code> to add hour and minute inputs below the calendar. The popover stays open after selecting a date so the user can adjust the time. Use arrow keys to increment or decrement values.</>} code={`<DatePicker
  showTime
  triggerDateFormat="MM/dd/yy HH:mm"
  placeholder="Select date & time"
  clearable pill
/>`} previewMinHeight={168}>
  <DatePickerTimeDemo />
</ComponentPreview>

### Dropdown + time

<ComponentPreview details={<>Combine <code>captionLayout="dropdown"</code> with <code>showTime</code> for quick month/year jumps and time selection in a single picker — useful for scheduling events at a specific date and time.</>} code={`<DatePicker
  captionLayout="dropdown"
  showTime
  yearRange={[2020, 2030]}
  triggerDateFormat="MMM d, yyyy HH:mm"
  placeholder="Schedule event"
  clearable pill
/>`} previewMinHeight={168}>
  <DatePickerDateTimeDropdownDemo />
</ComponentPreview>

### Two fields
