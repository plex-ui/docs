### Limits

<ComponentPreview details={<>Use <code>min</code> and <code>max</code> to restrict the date range. The mask prevents entering values outside the allowed range.</>} code={`<DateInput
  min={today.minus({ days: 30 }).startOf('day')}
  max={today.plus({ days: 30 }).endOf('day')}
  clearable
/>`} previewMinHeight={80}>
  <DateInputLimitsDemo />
</ComponentPreview>

### ISO format

### With Field

<ComponentPreview details={<>Wrap with <code>Field</code> to add a label, description, and automatic accessibility attributes.</>} code={`<Field label="Date of birth" description="Enter your date of birth">

</Field>`} previewMinHeight={100}>
  <DateInputWithFieldDemo />

### Validation

<ComponentPreview details={<>Pass <code>errorMessage</code> to <code>Field</code> to display a validation error.</>} code={`<Field label="Date of birth" errorMessage={!date ? 'Date is required' : undefined}>

</Field>`} previewMinHeight={100}>
  <DateInputWithFieldErrorDemo />
